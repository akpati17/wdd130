Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa and the most populous nation in Africa. Its capital city is Abuja, while Lagos is the largest city and main commercial center. Nigeria is known for its rich cultural diversity, with over 250 ethnic groups, including the Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. The country has abundant natural resources, especially petroleum, which plays a major role in its economy. Nigeria is also famous for its music, movies (Nollywood), literature, and strong religious life, with Christianity and Islam being the major religions.
